Anti-references — explicitly do NOT produce:
- Italian-language text anywhere.
- Catholic / religious iconography.
- Names "Amaro", "Gin", "Religin", "Cattaneo", "Della Nonna".
- Purple-as-primary-label-ground.
- WAX-DIPPED CAP — black plastic only.
- A wide / flask-shaped bottle.
- A 'C' letter on the badge — only the Ô.
- Radial wedges or stripes on the badge.
- A checkerboard pattern as the background.
- Modern Olympic-gymnastics register — strictly mid-century travelling-circus archive feel.
- Multiple acrobats on a single label — one figure only.
- Hand-painted illustration — only B&W photographic cutout.
