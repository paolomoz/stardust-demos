A delicate hand-engraved-feel logo of a big-top circus tent canvas seen from directly above, in monochrome (black and warm cream only). The wheel fills most of the square canvas — exactly 8 equal radial wedges, alternating between deep ink (hex #1a1410, the brand's noite color) and warm cream (hex #fffaef, the brand's cal color), like a top-down view of striped tent canvas in a vintage scratchboard / wood engraving register.

The line weight is INTENTIONALLY LIGHT — much thinner than a bold poster logo. The dark-ink spoke lines separating each wedge are HAIRLINES (think 0.5–1px equivalent at output size, not 2–3px). The outer ring is a SINGLE THIN hairline circle — no thick poster border. Where two cream wedges meet, only a hairline separates them. Where a noite wedge meets a cream wedge, the boundary is the edge of the noite fill (no extra line).

Centred on the wheel: NO inner hub disc — the centre is open, with the wedges meeting directly at a small filled noite dot at the exact centre. The italic capital letter "Ô" set in classical Bodoni-style display serif sits DIRECTLY at the centre of the wheel, large enough that the letter's bowl spans the wedge intersection. The Ô is in noite ink. The Ô's circumflex accent (^) above the O reads as the Portuguese diacritic and visually as a small flag rising from the tent's centre pole.

Three small six-pointed Bodoni-style star ornaments scattered on the CREAM wedges only, evenly distributed around the wheel at about 65% of the radius, in noite ink — small and delicate, not bold.

Image background: solid warm cream (#fffaef), flat — NOT a checkerboard / transparency-indicator pattern, NOT white, NOT gray.

Aesthetic: hand-engraved / scratchboard / fine-line letterpress feel. Delicate, sophisticated, REFINED rather than bold. The whole logo reads as if engraved into copper or scratched into a scratchboard, with thin precise lines throughout. NO thick poster lines. NO photographic texture. NO drop shadow.

Anti-references — explicitly do NOT produce:
- Photorealistic circus tent photography
- 3D rendering
- A logo with the letter "C" anywhere — only the Ô
- Any text other than the single letter "Ô"
- Italian-language words
- A pinwheel / windmill / cart-wheel reading
- Burgundy, peach-coral, mustard, teal, or orange — NO COLOR outside black and cream
- Pure white (use #fffaef cream)
- An inner hub disc — the centre is open
- THICK bold lines — this variant is intentionally LIGHT / DELICATE
- Roman Ô — must be italic
- More than three star ornaments
