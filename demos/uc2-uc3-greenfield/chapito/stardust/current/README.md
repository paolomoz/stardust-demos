- Highlighter-tape manifesto paragraph (phrase-spans wrapped in flat solid-color rectangles)
- Color-block product collage sections (×4, one per family, with cutout-photo overlays)
- Chip-tag cluster (circular cutout-photo chips with colored half-band labels)
- Big-dark bottle hero (single flagship bottle on deep-teal ground with wave-line motif)
- Circular wordmark footer
- Persistent thin bottom bar

The cultural register is **rotated to Lisbon + circus + Alentejo**. No Italian language, place, category, or brand reference appears in the implementation. The reference brand is a vocabulary of moves, not a template.
