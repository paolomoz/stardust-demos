## Voice cheat sheet (read first; apply every page)

**Narrator:** Inês Bento. First-person singular everywhere except legal/utility footer. Slow plainspoken declaratives. Periods over commas. Bilingual where bilingual makes sense; Portuguese is the brand's native language, English is the gloss.

**Anchor phrases:**

- *"Molhos com piruetas."*
- *"Casa pequena, picante grande."*
- *"Cada frasco, uma figura."*
- *"As etiquetas são barulhentas porque a cozinha é silenciosa."*
- *"Vem ao Chapitô."*
- *"O sol num frasco."*
- *"Trapézio sobre malagueta."*

**Forbidden:** *we, our team, passionate, crafted, curated, artisanal, handcrafted, saudade, fado, azulejo (as decoration), pastel de nata, bacalhau (as set-dressing), atelier, journal, dispatches.* No Italian-language phrases. No reference to Italy, Italian places, Italian liquor categories, or any specific Italian-pop liquor brand by name.
