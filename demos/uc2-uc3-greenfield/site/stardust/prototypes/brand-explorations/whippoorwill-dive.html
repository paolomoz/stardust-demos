A note from Junior —

The whippoorwill calls for one reason.

"The whippoorwill calls for one reason. To find another whippoorwill. That's about all the philosophy I can offer for what I do. I forage with a headlamp and I write product names on cocktail napkins. The herbs come from Madison County, the bottles come from a glass guy in Knoxville, the still I built myself in 2019."

"If you find one of these in a bar in Asheville, the bartender probably knows me, and the bartender probably knows what's in it. The labels are painted by my cousin who does jukebox cabinets."

— Junior
