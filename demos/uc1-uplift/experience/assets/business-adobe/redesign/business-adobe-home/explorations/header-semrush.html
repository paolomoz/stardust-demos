Header up + Adobe + Semrush in the hero — exploration

Three options for bringing the gnav to the top of the screen and folding the Adobe + Semrush acquisition announcement into the hero, instead of a separate strip above the gnav. All three keep the gnav contract intact and add only new page-level selectors (no module overrides — see MODULE-CONTENT-RULES.md).

Note: The real Adobe + Semrush SVG lockup at business.adobe.com/fragments/homepage/media_…svg is blocked by CDN bot-protection (HTTP/2 INTERNAL_ERROR; same as PIPELINE.md S8). The mocks below use a hand-built lockup approximation in the same horizontal arrangement. Once Playwright is wired up, the real asset drops in unchanged.
